About Me

From Gamer to Tester: Driven by Curiosity

My Story

It all started with video games. Beyond just playing them, I was fascinated by understanding how they worked, how those worlds were built, and the logic behind them. That technical curiosity was my first step into technology.

Later on, connecting with professionals in the field, I discovered the role of QA (Quality Assurance). I learned about their methodology and got hooked: it was the perfect balance between technical skills and analysis.

Today, my goal is clear: I don't settle for testing "on the surface." I am training to master code expertly. I firmly believe that to be an exceptional QA, one must understand how software is built piece by piece to effectively break it and improve it.
